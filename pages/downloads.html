Changelog

0.3.21

Bug fixes for video and network features

  • Fixed segfaults in libjpeg by avoiding to send junk to decoder
  • Adjusted net interface MTU to 1480 to avoid TCP stall

0.3.20

Video and IP protocol reliable transmission

  • Fixed video transmission mode
  • Data modem now uses continuous transmission
  • Fixed PSK modems phase errors due to equalizer

0.3.18

  • VOX mode
  • SSB and AM improvements

0.3.16

  • FM, wide FM, SSB receiver improvements
  • Added USB and LSB modes to SSB transmitter

0.3.15

  • Modem and performance improvements

0.3.6

  • Full duplex mode: analog and digital repeater
  • FreeDV
