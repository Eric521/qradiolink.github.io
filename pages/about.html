About

QRadioLink

QRadioLink is a GUI for a collection of Gnuradio modems, built for hobbyists, tinkerers and radio enthusiasts, which allows experimenting with SDR hardware using different digital and analog modes.

It can also be used as a SDR transceiver for amateur radio.

Possible applications:

  • digital walkie talkie
  • baby monitor
  • video camera streaming
  • IoT devices
  • radio LAN
  • robotics

Features

  • Receives and transmits analog voice, digital voice, low resolution video, text, IP protocol.
  • Narrow band modem with Codec2 or wideband modem and Opus.
  • Digital Modems: BPSK QPSK 2FSK 4FSK
  • Modes: narrow FM, SSB, digital voice, digital video, digital data
  • Formats: Codec2 700B, Codec2 1400, Opus 10 kbit/s
  • Video formats: JPEG
  • Supported hardware: Ettus USRP, RTL-SDR, HackRF, BladeRF and in general all devices supported by gr-osmosdr

Typical receiver performance

Mode Condition Sensitivity (dBm)
Codec2 700B 20 db SINAD -115
Codec2 1400 20 db SINAD -112
Opus 20 db SINAD -102
Narrow FM 12 db SINAD -118

Measured on an RTL-SDR USB stick, R820T tuner.
